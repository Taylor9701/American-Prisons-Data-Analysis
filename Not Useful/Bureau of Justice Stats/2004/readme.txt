Prisoners in 2004, NCJ 210677
 
This zip archive contains tables in individual .csv spreadsheets
from Prisoners in 2004, NCJ 210677. The full report including 
text and graphics in pdf format are available from:
http://www.ojp.usdoj.gov/bjs/abstract/p04.htm

This report is one in a series.  More recent editions
may be available.  To view a list of all in the series go to
http://www.ojp.usdoj.gov/bjs/pubalp2.htm#Prisoners
